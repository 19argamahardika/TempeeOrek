Tempe Orek

A Delicious Indonesian Dish

Description

Tempe Orek is a popular Indonesian dish made from fried tempeh mixed with a sweet and savory seasoning. It is simple, flavorful, and perfect as a side dish.

Purpose

The purpose of making Tempe Orek is to create an easy, nutritious, and tasty meal that complements various traditional Indonesian dishes.

Ingredients

  • 1 block of tempeh, cut into matchsticks
  • 5 shallots
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 3 curly red chilies
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3 tbsp palm sugar
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • Sweet soy sauce to taste
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Slice the tempeh, shallots, garlic, and chillies.
  2. Fry the tempeh until crispy.
  3. Saute the shallots and garlic until golden. Add bay leaves, palm sugar, pepper, and a little bit of salt. Saute until the palm sugar melt.
  4. Add the tempeh and mix all the ingredients.
  5. Delicious tempe orek is ready to serve!.
